Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Seasons of Harmony. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile game.

2. Information We Collect

Seasons of Harmony is designed to work completely offline. We collect minimal information:

  • Game Progress Data: Your game progress, achievements, and settings are stored locally on your device only.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Location Data: We do not access or collect your device location.
  • No Camera/Microphone: We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts.

3. How We Use Information

The game progress data stored locally on your device is used solely to:

  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Remember your game settings and preferences
  • Track achievements and unlocked content

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All game data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by Android.
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not automatically back up your data to the cloud.
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored game data.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. We do not integrate with social media platforms or other external services.

6. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data collection from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

7. Advertising

Seasons of Harmony is a premium, paid game with no advertisements. We do not display ads or collect advertising identifiers.

8. In-App Purchases

If you make optional in-app purchases, these transactions are processed by Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle payment data.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your game data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your game data (by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in device settings)
  • Request information about our privacy practices

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Data Protection (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not collect personal data, most GDPR obligations do not apply. However, you retain the right to contact us with any privacy concerns.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

For California residents, we do not sell personal information. Since we do not collect personal information, CCPA rights regarding data access and deletion are not applicable, but you may contact us with any questions.
